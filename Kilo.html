How to play Kilo?

You score points if your choice of either 'Higher' or 'Lower' matches.
You will score a point on 'Lower' if the next card is lower than the current one, and will score on 'Higher' if it's equal or higher.
Cards have values corresponding to the number on them; as for Ace, Joker, Queen and King it's quite different however. Since Ace is equal to 11 (yes, there's no card with value of 1, we start with value of 2 in this game),
the other three cards are usually understood as *more than 10, but less than 11*. They go in this order: J < Q < K.
